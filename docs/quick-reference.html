Energy Rules

  • Starting reserve: 2 energy
  • Maximum reserve: 4 energy
  • Regeneration: +1 energy per turn (end of turn)
  • Moves cost energy on use, regen happens after

Top 5 Strategic Tips

  1. Power Strike is king. It deals nearly double Strike damage and ignores Guard. Always plan your energy around landing Power Strikes.
  2. Count the CPU's energy. If it just Power Struck, it has low reserves and must Hold or Strike. Safe to attack without guarding.
  3. Guard baits Strikes. Using Guard makes the CPU more likely to Strike next turn. Use this to predict and punish.
  4. Hold is not wasted. Holding when the opponent Guards means you saved energy while they burned 1. Net gain for you.
  5. Ancient tier = best grind. Triceratops and Carnotaurus can face Excavated opponents (easy wins) while still being strong enough to beat Apex dinos with good play.
