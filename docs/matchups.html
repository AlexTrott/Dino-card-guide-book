Key Takeaways

Giganotosaurus can one-shot Velociraptor, Stegosaurus, and Dilophosaurus with a single Strike. Iguanodon and Parasaurolophus deal only 1 damage per Strike against heavily armored targets — they would need 90 to 150 turns to KO an Ankylosaurus or Giganotosaurus through Strikes alone. In those matchups, Power Strike becomes absolutely essential.

Tier Rankings

Based on overall stat totals, matchup spread, and competitive viability, here is the definitive DinoCard tier list. Keep in mind that rarer cards are harder to obtain, and underdog strategies can upset the rankings.

S Overpowered
Giganotosaurus Extinction
ATK 110 / DEF 70 / HP 150 — Best stats in every single category. Nothing comes close. The only balancing factor is its brutal 2% drop rate. If you pull one, you win.
A Dominant
T-Rex Apex
ATK 95 / DEF 60 / HP 130 — Near-perfect stats across the board, the king's rival. One-shots low-tier dinos and can trade blows with Giganotosaurus.
Spinosaurus Apex
ATK 85 / DEF 55 / HP 120 — A true powerhouse with great HP. Its massive health pool lets it survive exchanges that would fell lesser dinos.
B Strong
Triceratops Ancient
ATK 60 / DEF 65 / HP 100 — The best all-rounder in the game. Solid ATK, excellent DEF, and a beefy HP pool. No glaring weaknesses make it a reliable anchor for any team.
Carnotaurus Ancient
ATK 75 / DEF 35 / HP 85 — A devastating glass cannon. Highest ATK in the mid-tiers but paper-thin defense. Hits like a truck but crumbles under pressure.
C Viable
Ankylosaurus Excavated
ATK 35 / DEF 70 / HP 90 — An incredible DEF wall. Its 70 DEF is tied for the highest in the game, making it nearly impossible for low-ATK dinos to scratch. Lacks offensive punch.
Dilophosaurus Excavated
ATK 55 / DEF 30 / HP 65 — Strong ATK for its tier and surprisingly dangerous. Fragile with low HP and DEF, but its damage output can catch opponents off guard.
D Underpowered
Stegosaurus Fossilized
ATK 30 / DEF 50 / HP 70 — A balanced common card with decent DEF but underwhelming ATK. Serviceable as a starter but outclassed by everything above it.
Velociraptor Fossilized
ATK 40 / DEF 25 / HP 60 — A glass cannon common card. Decent ATK for its rarity but fragile with the lowest HP and DEF in the game. Lives fast, dies young.
F Outclassed
Parasaurolophus Fossilized
ATK 25 / DEF 40 / HP 85 — Tanky but no damage. Its high HP lets it survive, but with only 25 ATK it deals 1 damage per Strike to armored targets. Stalling incarnate.
Iguanodon Fossilized
ATK 20 / DEF 45 / HP 80 — A wall with no punch. Highest DEF among commons but the lowest ATK in the entire game. Needs 150 Strikes to KO a Giganotosaurus. Avoid in competitive play.
