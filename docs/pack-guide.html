Pack Opening Guide

Rarity Drop Rates

Every pack contains 5 cards. Each card slot is rolled independently using weighted random selection. Here are the exact drop rates pulled from the game's source code:

Rarity Weight Drop Rate Avg Cards per 10 Packs
Fossilized 55 55% ~27.5
Excavated 25 25% ~12.5
Ancient 12 12% ~6
Apex 6 6% ~3
Extinction 2 2% ~1

What This Means In Practice

Cards Per Rarity Tier

Within each rarity tier, the specific dinosaur is chosen uniformly at random:

Rarity Dinos in Tier Chance per Specific Dino
Fossilized 3 55% ÷ 3 = 18.3%
Excavated 3 25% ÷ 3 = 8.3%
Ancient 2 12% ÷ 2 = 6%
Apex 2 6% ÷ 2 = 3%
Extinction 1 2% ÷ 1 = 2%
The Giganotosaurus Problem

With only a 2% chance per card slot and 5 slots per pack, the probability of getting at least one Giganotosaurus in a single pack is:

P = 1 - (0.98)^5 = 1 - 0.9039 = 9.6%

That's roughly 1 in 10 packs. On average, you'll need to open about 10-11 packs to pull a Giganotosaurus. Since you earn 1 pack per battle win, plan on winning at least 10 battles!

Guaranteed Rarity System

DinoCard has a built-in pity mechanic to prevent truly awful pulls. Here's how it works:

The Pity Rule

The game checks the first 4 cards in your pack. If none of them are Excavated or higher, the 5th card is guaranteed to be at least Excavated rarity.

This means: every pack has at least one uncommon+ card.

Pity System Probability

What's the chance of the pity system activating? The first 4 cards must ALL be Fossilized:

P(all 4 common) = (0.55)^4 = 9.15%

So roughly 1 in 11 packs will trigger the pity system. The other 10+ packs naturally contain at least one uncommon or better card without needing the safety net.

Trainer Tip: Pity Card Quality
When the pity system activates, the 5th card re-rolls its rarity but only from the Excavated+ pool. The relative weights become:
Rarity Original Weight Pity Adjusted Rate
Excavated 25 25/45 = 55.6%
Ancient 12 12/45 = 26.7%
Apex 6 6/45 = 13.3%
Extinction 2 2/45 = 4.4%

The pity card actually has a higher chance of being Ancient or above compared to a normal pull! So a "bad" pack might reward you with a surprise rare.

Collection Completion Estimates

How many packs do you need to collect all 11 dinosaurs? Here's a rough estimate based on probability:

Goal Expected Packs Expected Battle Wins
All 3 Fossilized dinos ~3-5 ~3-5
All 3 Excavated dinos ~5-8 ~5-8
Both Ancient dinos ~8-12 ~8-12
Both Apex dinos ~15-25 ~15-25
Giganotosaurus ~10-15 ~10-15
Complete Collection (all 11) ~25-40 ~25-40
Trainer Tip: Efficient Pack Grinding

The fastest way to complete your collection:

  1. Open your initial packs to get some Fossilized cards
  2. Battle with your best card to earn more packs
  3. As you get stronger cards, switch to battling with them
  4. Use Ancient-tier cards for the best win-rate-to-matchmaking ratio
  5. Keep opening packs until Giganotosaurus appears!
