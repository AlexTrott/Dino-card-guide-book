Battle Strategy

AN ABSOLUTE POWERHOUSE. 85 ATK, 55 DEF, 120 HP — no weaknesses. Strike = max(1, 68−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 127.5−DEF×0.15). Against T-Rex (60 DEF): Strike = max(1, 68−18) = 50, Power Strike = max(1, 127.5−9) = 118.

You can nearly one-shot T-Rex with a Power Strike! Play a balanced offense — mix Strikes and Power Strikes to keep your energy flowing while maintaining constant pressure.

Best Matchup
Anything below Apex tier. You simply overpower them. Ancient and Excavated tier dinosaurs cannot match your stat line — 85 ATK tears through their defenses while your 120 HP absorbs everything they throw at you.
Watch Out!
T-Rex edges you out in every stat. 10 more ATK, 5 more DEF, 10 more HP — it beats you in the Apex mirror match more often than not. If you face T-Rex, you need smart energy management and well-timed Guards to close the gap.
