Battle Strategy

A SERIOUS THREAT. 75 ATK makes every hit count. Strike = max(1, 60−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 112.5−DEF×0.15). Against Triceratops (65 DEF): Strike = max(1, 60−19.5) = 40, Power Strike = max(1, 112.5−9.75) = 102.

You can two-shot most dinosaurs with Power Strikes. Play aggressive — Hold turn 1, Power Strike turn 2. Your 75 ATK tears through anything that doesn’t have elite-tier DEF.

Best Matchup
Any herbivore with low ATK. You destroy them before they can mount a meaningful response. Your 75 ATK Power Strikes deal massive damage, and low-ATK opponents can’t punish your thin 35 DEF quickly enough.
Watch Out!
Triceratops is your worst nightmare. Its 65 DEF neuters your Strikes, and it has 100 HP to outlast your 85 HP. Your paper-thin 35 DEF means high-ATK opponents shred through you — if you can’t end the fight fast, you lose.
