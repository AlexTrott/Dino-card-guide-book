Armored plates and a powerful tail make this herbivore a tough opponent

Battle Strategy

The best-balanced common. 50 DEF is the highest at Fossilized tier and even competitive with Excavated dinos. Strike = max(1, 24−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 45−DEF×0.15).

Against Velociraptor (25 DEF): Strike = 16, Power Strike = 41. Has better offensive output than Iguanodon while maintaining strong defense.

Best Matchup

Velociraptor — high DEF plus decent ATK equals a comfortable win. Your 50 DEF shrugs off Velociraptor's Strikes while you deal meaningful damage in return.

Watch Out!

Lower HP than Iguanodon (70 vs 80). Not as tanky overall despite higher DEF. Ankylosaurus is your worst nightmare when matched up — its 70 DEF makes your Strikes deal 1 damage.
