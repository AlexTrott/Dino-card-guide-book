Battle Strategy

THE ULTIMATE CARD. 110 ATK, 70 DEF, 150 HP — the highest in every category. Strike = max(1, 88−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 165−DEF×0.15). Against T-Rex (60 DEF): Strike = max(1, 88−18) = 70, Power Strike = max(1, 165−9) = 156.

You one-shot T-Rex with Power Strike! With a 2% drop rate, this is the rarest and most powerful card in the game. Play however you want — you’re the boss. No strategy required when you have the highest stats in every single category.

Best Matchup
Everything. You have the highest stats in every category. No dinosaur in the game can match your 110 ATK, 70 DEF, and 150 HP. Every matchup is favorable — you are the apex of the apex.
Watch Out!
T-Rex is the closest thing to a counter. Due to matchmaking, you only face Apex or Extinction tier opponents. T-Rex’s 95 ATK hurts even through your 70 DEF — its Power Strike deals 132 damage. With smart play and good energy management, a T-Rex player can still put up a real fight. Don’t get careless.
