Parasaurolophus

Parasaurolophus

Excavated (Uncommon) Herbivore
ATK
25
DEF
40

Its haunting crest call echoes across the battlefield, unsettling opponents

Battle Strategy

A pure tank with staying power. 85 HP at Excavated tier is impressive, and 40 DEF is decent. The problem is 25 ATK — second worst in the game. Strike = max(1, 20−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 37.5−DEF×0.15).

You need to fight patiently. Guard to reduce damage and save energy, then Power Strike for your only meaningful damage output. Don’t bother with regular Strikes against anything with decent DEF — they’ll deal near-minimum damage.

Best Matchup

Iguanodon — low-ATK mirrors where both sides deal minimal damage. In these wars of attrition, your superior 85 HP pool gives you the edge. You’ll outlast them simply by having more health to burn through.

Watch Out!

Terrible ATK (25). You struggle to actually KO anything in a reasonable time. Any carnivore out-damages you massively — their high ATK tears through your 40 DEF while your 25 ATK barely registers against them.
