20

45

Gentle giant with a devastating thumb spike for defense

Battle Strategy

The ultimate wall at common tier. With 45 DEF, enemy Strikes are heavily reduced. Against Velociraptor Strike: max(1, 32−13.5) = 18 damage to you, but your 80 HP absorbs it. Your own damage is pathetic though — 20 ATK means Strike = max(1, 16−DEF×0.3).

Against Velociraptor (25 DEF): Strike = max(1, 16−7.5) = 8. You need Power Strike to win: max(1, 30−DEF×0.15). Guard aggressively, save energy, then Power Strike when you can.

Best Matchup

Velociraptor — you absorb its damage easily while slowly wearing it down. Your 80 HP and 45 DEF make you nearly impervious to its Strikes.

Watch Out!

Atrocious ATK (20). Battles will be LONG — some matchups take 15+ turns. Against any high-DEF dino, you literally deal 1 damage per Strike. Patience is mandatory when piloting Iguanodon.
