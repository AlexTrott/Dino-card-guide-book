A living fortress with a devastating club tail

Battle Strategy

THE WALL. 70 DEF is tied with Giganotosaurus for the highest in the entire game. Enemy Strikes do almost nothing. Against a 40-ATK Velociraptor: Strike = max(1, 32−21) = 11. That’s barely a scratch.

Your weakness is your low ATK (35). Your Strike = max(1, 28−DEF×0.3). Use Guard liberally to waste enemy energy, then punish with Power Strike (52.5−DEF×0.15). You win through patience and attrition.

Best Matchup

Any low-ATK dino — Iguanodon and Parasaurolophus deal minimum damage to you. Their Strikes bounce right off your 70 DEF while you slowly grind them down with Power Strikes.

Watch Out!

Painfully low ATK (35). Matches drag on forever. High-ATK Power Strike spammers are your nightmare — Power Strike bypasses your amazing DEF advantage (only reduced by DEF×0.15 instead of DEF×0.3). You win by attrition, not aggression.
