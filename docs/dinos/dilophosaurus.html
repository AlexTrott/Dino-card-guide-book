Dilophosaurus

Dilophosaurus
Excavated Carnivore
ATK
55
DEF
30
HP
65

Venomous predator that strikes with blinding speed and toxic fury

Battle Strategy

A glass cannon. 55 ATK is very high for Excavated tier. Strike = max(1, 44−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 82.5−DEF×0.15). Against Ankylosaurus (70 DEF): Strike = max(1, 44−21) = 23, Power Strike = max(1, 82.5−10.5) = 72. You can actually break through even the toughest armor.

But 65 HP and 30 DEF mean you crumble fast under return fire. Go all-out offense. Hold then Power Strike is your bread and butter. Don’t waste turns Guarding — you can’t afford a long fight.

Best Matchup

Parasaurolophus — your 55 ATK vs its 40 DEF deals great damage, while its pathetic 25 ATK barely scratches you even with your low 30 DEF. This is a comfortable win if you stay aggressive.

Watch Out!

Second lowest HP in the game (65). One Power Strike from most dinosaurs chunks you hard. Against Carnotaurus or higher (75+ ATK), your 65 HP evaporates in just a few turns. You must kill them before they kill you — and with those stats against you, that’s a tall order.
