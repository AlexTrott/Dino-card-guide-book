Velociraptor

Velociraptor

Fossilized Carnivore
ATK
40
DEF
25
HP
60

Swift and cunning pack hunter with razor-sharp claws

Battle Strategy

Glass cannon at the lowest tier. Low HP means you must be aggressive. Strike is your bread and butter — at 40 ATK and 0.8x multiplier = 32 base, reduced by opponent DEF. Against other Fossilized dinos it does decent damage. Power Strike does 40×1.5 = 60 minus DEF×0.15.

Against Iguanodon (45 DEF): Strike = max(1, 32−13.5) = 18, Power Strike = max(1, 60−6.75) = 53. Against Stegosaurus (50 DEF): Strike = max(1, 32−15) = 17, Power Strike = max(1, 60−7.5) = 52.

Best Matchup

Iguanodon — its low 20 ATK means Iguanodon barely hurts you, giving you time to land Power Strikes and close out the fight.

Watch Out!

Terrible HP (60) and DEF (25). A single Power Strike from most dinos is devastating. Stegosaurus is your worst matchup — its higher DEF reduces your already modest damage, and it has more HP to outlast you.
