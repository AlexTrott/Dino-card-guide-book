Battle Strategy

THE BEST ALL-ROUNDER. 60 ATK is respectable, 65 DEF is second only to Ankylosaurus and Giganotosaurus, and 100 HP gives great staying power. Strike = max(1, 48−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 90−DEF×0.15).

Against Carnotaurus (35 DEF): Strike = 37, Power Strike = 84. A comfortable matchup. Triceratops excels because it has no glaring weakness — solid ATK, excellent DEF, and above-average HP make it reliable in any situation.

Best Matchup
Carnotaurus. Your 65 DEF tanks its 75 ATK Strikes remarkably well, and you have 100 HP to its 85 HP — you outlast it every time. Carnotaurus’s paper-thin 35 DEF means your hits land hard.
Watch Out!
Jack of all trades, master of none. Triceratops doesn’t dominate in any single stat. Higher-tier cards like T-Rex and Spinosaurus simply overpower your defenses with their superior ATK. You’re the best at your rarity, but you won’t win raw stat battles against Apex or Extinction tier opponents.
