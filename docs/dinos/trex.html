Battle Strategy

THE SECOND MOST POWERFUL CARD IN THE GAME. 95 ATK is devastating. Strike = max(1, 76−DEF×0.3), Power Strike = max(1, 142.5−DEF×0.15). Against Giganotosaurus (70 DEF): Strike = max(1, 76−21) = 55, Power Strike = max(1, 142.5−10.5) = 132.

You can challenge even the legendary Giganotosaurus! 130 HP means you survive multiple hits. Go aggressive with Power Strikes — your raw ATK overwhelms nearly everything in the game.

Best Matchup
Everything except Giganotosaurus. You are the king of Apex tier. Spinosaurus, Carnotaurus, Triceratops — none of them can match your 95 ATK / 60 DEF / 130 HP stat line. You dominate every matchup at your tier and below.
Watch Out!
Giganotosaurus is your only true counter. 15 more ATK, 10 more DEF, 20 more HP — it outclasses you in every category. You may face it due to ±1 rarity matchmaking. If you do, play perfectly: time your Guards, manage energy, and hope for some luck.
